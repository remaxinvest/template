About Us

Welcome to The Stables Restaurant! We offer a delightful dining experience with a variety of delicious breakfast and starter options. Enjoy our popular Bacon Roll, flavorful Baguettes, and many more dishes made with fresh ingredients. We are open from 10:00 AM to 9:00 PM, serving breakfast from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
